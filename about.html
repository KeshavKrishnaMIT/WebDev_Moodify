About Moodify

Moodify is a simple and interactive web app that helps you choose the right activity based on your mood. Whether you feel happy, chill, energetic, sad, or focused — Moodify guides you to something that matches your vibe.

How it works

  • Select your current mood
  • Choose what you want to do — listen, watch, read, or play
  • Get an instant suggestion that fits your mood

Why Moodify?

Moodify is built to be fast, clean, and fun. It is designed as a learning project and a creative experiment to explore how mood-based recommendations can improve digital experiences.
